# Professional Educational Color Palette
## EduPath Scholarship Website

### 🎨 Color Swatches & Hex Codes

#### PRIMARY COLORS - Deep Academic Blue (Trust, Stability, Knowledge)
- **Primary Base**: `#1E3A8A` - Main brand color, conveys authority and trust
- **Primary Light**: `#3B82F6` - Lighter variant for backgrounds and accents
- **Primary Dark**: `#1E40AF` - Darker variant for borders and emphasis
- **Primary Hover**: `#1D4ED8` - Interactive state color

#### SECONDARY COLORS - Muted Green (Growth, Opportunity, Progress)
- **Secondary Base**: `#2F855A` - Represents growth and opportunity
- **Secondary Light**: `#4CAF50` - Brighter variant for success states
- **Secondary Dark**: `#276749` - Deeper variant for emphasis
- **Secondary Hover**: `#38A169` - Interactive state color

#### ACCENT COLORS - Prestige Gold (Call-to-Actions, Highlights)
- **Accent Base**: `#FBBF24` - Primary CTA color, high visibility
- **Accent Light**: `#FFD700` - Brighter gold for special highlights
- **Accent Dark**: `#F59E0B` - Darker variant for depth
- **Accent Hover**: `#D97706` - Interactive state (darker gold/orange)

#### TERTIARY COLORS - Soft Teal (Variety, Freshness)
- **Tertiary Base**: `#0D9488` - Fresh accent color for variety
- **Tertiary Light**: `#14B8A6` - Lighter variant for backgrounds
- **Tertiary Dark**: `#0F766E` - Deeper variant for emphasis

#### NEUTRAL COLORS - Clean Backgrounds & Text
- **Pure White**: `#FFFFFF` - Primary background color
- **Light Grey**: `#F4F4F6` - Secondary background for sections
- **Soft Grey**: `#F9FAFB` - Tertiary background for cards
- **Dark Grey**: `#1F2937` - Primary text color (instead of pure black)
- **Medium Grey**: `#4B5563` - Secondary text color
- **Light Text**: `#6B7280` - Muted text color
- **Subtle Grey**: `#9CA3AF` - Light text for less important content

#### LINK COLORS
- **Link Default**: `#2563EB` - Standard link color (blue)
- **Link Hover**: `#0D9488` - Hover state (teal accent)
- **Link Visited**: `#7C3AED` - Visited link color (purple)

### 🎯 Usage Guidelines

#### Header & Footer
- **Background**: Deep Academic Blue (`#1E3A8A`)
- **Text**: Pure White (`#FFFFFF`)
- **Accent Elements**: Prestige Gold (`#FBBF24`)

#### Main Content Areas
- **Background**: Pure White (`#FFFFFF`) or Light Grey (`#F4F4F6`)
- **Text**: Dark Grey (`#1F2937`)
- **Section Dividers**: Muted Green (`#2F855A`) or Soft Teal (`#0D9488`)

#### Call-to-Action Buttons
- **Primary CTA**: Prestige Gold (`#FBBF24`)
- **Hover State**: Darker Gold (`#D97706`)
- **Secondary CTA**: Muted Green (`#2F855A`)
- **Text on Buttons**: Pure White (`#FFFFFF`)

#### Cards & Components
- **Background**: Pure White (`#FFFFFF`)
- **Border**: Light border color (`#E5E7EB`)
- **Shadow**: Subtle shadows with low opacity
- **Hover Effects**: Lift with enhanced shadow

#### Status & Feedback Colors
- **Success**: Muted Green (`#2F855A`)
- **Warning**: Accent Dark (`#F59E0B`)
- **Error**: Red (`#DC2626`)
- **Info**: Primary Light (`#3B82F6`)

### 🌟 Design Philosophy

#### Mood & Tone
- **Formal & Professional**: Academic blue establishes credibility
- **Hopeful & Inspiring**: Green represents growth and opportunity
- **Prestigious & Trustworthy**: Gold conveys value and achievement
- **Clean & Modern**: White backgrounds ensure clarity and focus

#### Psychological Impact
- **Blue**: Trust, stability, intelligence, academic excellence
- **Green**: Growth, opportunity, progress, positive outcomes
- **Gold**: Prestige, value, achievement, success
- **White/Grey**: Clarity, professionalism, clean design

#### Accessibility Considerations
- **High Contrast**: Dark grey text on white backgrounds
- **Color Blind Friendly**: Distinct hues that work for all users
- **WCAG Compliant**: Meets accessibility standards for text contrast
- **Readable Typography**: Optimized color combinations for all users

### 🎨 Gradient Combinations

#### Subtle Backgrounds
- **Light Gradient**: `linear-gradient(135deg, #FFFFFF 0%, #F4F4F6 100%)`
- **Soft Gradient**: `linear-gradient(135deg, #F9FAFB 0%, #F4F4F6 100%)`

### 📱 Responsive Considerations

#### Mobile Optimization
- Maintain color contrast ratios across all screen sizes
- Ensure touch targets have adequate color differentiation
- Use color coding consistently across breakpoints

#### Dark Mode Alternative (Optional Future Enhancement)
- Primary: Lighter blue variants
- Backgrounds: Dark navy/charcoal
- Text: Light greys and whites
- Maintain brand recognition while adapting to dark themes
